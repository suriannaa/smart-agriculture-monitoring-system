The project explores how technology can support better irrigation decisions, plant monitoring, and sustainable farming practices.

## Longer Case Study Copy

This final year project focuses on a Smart Agriculture Monitoring System designed to support smarter and more sustainable farming practices. The system concept centers on monitoring important agricultural readings such as soil moisture and environmental conditions, presenting the information through a dashboard, and supporting users with features such as automatic irrigation, notifications, trend viewing, and fertilizer recommendation.

My work included preparing the project documentation, structuring the system modules, creating the evaluation materials, analysing survey responses, and presenting the project through a formal report, manuscript, and exhibition poster.
